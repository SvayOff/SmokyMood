FAQ'S

Answers to frequently asked questions

Here we answer the questions most often asked by our visitors, you can also ask us your question

  • What are the qualities of an experienced hookah maker?

    An experienced hookah maker must have the skills of a psychologist to help predict the actions and words of visitors. The way out of any situation will be complete calm and balance.

  • How to light a hookah

    If the session is planned at home, then first prepare the installation according to all the rules. Once the rig has warmed up, there's an easy way to check it's ready for a session - blow it. If light smoke comes out of the bowl, then you can start smoking.

  • How to smoke a hookah: in a puff or not?

    Inhaling should be done to completely fill the lungs, and then completely release the smoke from them. It can be smoked in a puff. This should be done according to the principle of inhaling air, and exhaling in any other convenient way.

  • When you smoke hookah, do you have to inhale?

    People with little experience using hookah are advised to inhale smoke slowly and carefully. Do not inhale a lot of smoke when inhaling. Smoking should not create a feeling of discomfort. Beginners are not recommended to smoke strong tobacco.

  • Good health during the session

    The main rule is to refrain from drinking strong alcoholic beverages. The combination of "hookah + alcohol" will cause a headache. One glass of wine will not affect your well-being, but larger doses of alcohol will definitely leave unpleasant feelings after (and during) the session.

  • How much smoke should there be from a hookah

    It is reasonable that the more coal or more heat is applied to the hookah, the more smoke will be produced. But you have to do it carefully to keep the smoke soft and as pleasant to the taste as possible

  • How to properly smoke hookah for the first time?

    To light a hookah for the first time, it is necessary to inhale the smoke from the finished bowl gradually, without making much effort. If there is very little smoke, it means that the tobacco has not yet warmed up, put the hookah aside for a couple of minutes

  • When is the best time to smoke hookah?

    Hookah smoking is a magical ritual shrouded in mystery. It helps people to relax as much as possible and get special relaxation.

  • What do you need to smoke hookah?

    A high-quality and reliable hookah is a central smoking device in any setting. Therefore, it is necessary to pay special attention to its configuration and assembly quality. For example, basic models often contain only half of the accessories necessary for proper and comfortable smoking.

  • Why do people smoke hookah?

    Hookah bars are often visited for relaxation and entertainment. In such places, people pass the hookah to each other while they joke and laugh. This makes such meetings warm and friendly. The sweet aroma of hookah also arouses curiosity in women and makes them try it at least once.
